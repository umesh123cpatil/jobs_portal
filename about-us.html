About us


IndiaJobs is a platform that connects job seekers and employers. Searching for jobs and applying for jobs on IndiaJobs.org is free for all job seekers. After applying, IndiaJobs sends all applications to the employers. Now, it is up to the employer who posted the job to schedule an interview with you.
